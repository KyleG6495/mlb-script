📝 TROUBLESHOOTING:
==================
Q: "No player_id column found"
A: Your fd_slate_today.csv needs an 'Id' column or 'player_id' column

Q: "No projection files found"  
A: Make sure fd_hitter_features_final.csv and fd_pitcher_features_final.csv exist

Q: "Optimization failed"
A: Check that your slate has players in all required positions (P, C, 1B, 2B, 3B, SS, OF)

Q: "Which lineups should I submit?"
A: Start with BALANCED lineups. Use FLOOR for cash games, CEILING for GPPs.
