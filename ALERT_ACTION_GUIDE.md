```

SEVERITY: 5/5 (CRITICAL)
```

### **EXACTLY What You Do:**

- Go to FanDuel.com or open the FanDuel app
- Log into your account
- Click "My Contests" or "Lineups"

#### **Step 2: Find Affected Lineups**
- Look for lineups named: `tournament_1`, `tournament_3`, `tournament_7`

- Click "Edit Lineup" on each one

#### **Step 3: Make the Swap**
- Click on Byron Buxton in the lineup
- Search for "Juan Soto" in the replacement box
- Click "Juan Soto" to select him
- Verify salary cap is still good (+$300 means you're fine)
- Click "Save Changes"

#### **Step 4: Repeat for All Lineups**
- Do this for all 3 affected lineups
- Double-check each save was successful

#### **Step 5: Confirm**
- Go back to your system console
- You should see: `✅ Swap recommendation executed`

## 📱 **STEP-BY-STEP FANDUEL TUTORIAL**

### **Finding Your Lineups:**
1. **FanDuel Website**: Click "My Contests" → "View Lineups"
2. **FanDuel App**: Tap "Contests" → "My Lineups"
3. **Search**: Use Ctrl+F (or search) for player names

### **Editing a Lineup:**
1. **Find the lineup** with the player you need to swap
2. **Click "Edit Lineup"** (or pencil icon)
3. **Click on the player** you want to replace
4. **Search for replacement** in the search box
5. **Click the new player** to select them
6. **Verify salary cap** (should show green checkmark)
7. **Click "Save Changes"** or "Update Lineup"

### **Verifying Your Changes:**
1. **Check lineup shows new player** name
2. **Verify salary is under $35,000**
3. **Confirm all positions filled**
4. **Look for green "Valid Lineup" indicator**
