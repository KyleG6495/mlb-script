# 🏆 Elite DFS Dashboard - User Guide

## 📖 How to Read Your DFS Dashboard

### **Quick Start - What to Look at First:**

#### **1. 👥 Real Ownership Tab (Start Here!)**
- **Top Metrics Row:**
  - `Chalk Plays` = High ownership players (>25%) - Consider fading in tournaments
  - `Contrarian Targets` = Low ownership players (<8%) - Great for GPP leverage
  - `Value Plays` = Best points per dollar ratio
  - `Leverage Spots` = Low ownership + high projection = tournament gold

- **Player Table:**
  - `Player` = Name
  - `Salary` = DraftKings/FanDuel cost
  - `Projection` = Expected fantasy points
  - `Ownership%` = How many people will play them
  - `Leverage` = Higher number = better tournament play
  - `Tier` = Chalk/Contrarian/Value classification

**💡 What to Do:** Look for players with high projection + low ownership!

---

#### **2. 💎 Real Opportunities Tab (Your Money Makers)**
This shows you the **actual plays** to make:

- **🔥 High Leverage Opportunities** = These are your tournament winners
  - Low ownership but high projections
  - These can win you contests

- **🎭 Contrarian Targets** = Fade the public
  - Players the public is avoiding
  - Great for differentiation

- **⚠️ High Ownership Chalk** = Consider avoiding
  - Everyone will have these players
  - Hard to win tournaments with them

**💡 What to Do:** Focus on the High Leverage section for your core plays!

---

#### **3. ⚡ Real Stacks Tab (Team Correlations)**
- **Team stacks** = Multiple players from same team
- When a team scores a lot, all their hitters benefit
- Look at the `Value` column for best bang for buck

**💡 What to Do:** Pick 3-4 players from the top teams in the table

---

#### **4. 🎯 Elite Lineups Tab (Ready-Made Lineups)**
These are **complete lineups** optimized by your system:
- `Rank` = Best to worst
- `Projection` = Expected total points
- `Ownership%` = How popular this lineup will be
- Lower ownership = better for tournaments

**💡 What to Do:** Copy lineup #1 for cash games, lineup with lowest ownership% for tournaments

---

### **🎯 Simple Strategy Guide:**

#### **For Cash Games (50/50s, Double-Ups):**
1. Go to **Elite Lineups tab**
2. Use the **top ranked lineup** (highest projection)
3. Focus on players with 15-30% ownership (safe but good)

#### **For Tournaments (GPPs):**
1. Go to **Real Opportunities tab**
2. Pick 2-3 players from **High Leverage Opportunities**
3. Go to **Real Stacks tab** 
4. Pick a stack with low ownership% but high projection
5. Fill remaining spots with contrarian targets

#### **For Late Swap:**
1. Go to **Late Swap tab** 
2. Check for any injury alerts
3. Have backup players ready
4. Monitor 2 hours before games

---

### **🚦 Traffic Light System:**

#### **🟢 GREEN = GOOD PLAYS**
- High projection + Low ownership
- High leverage score (>0.75)
- Value plays with good points/dollar

#### **🟡 YELLOW = PROCEED WITH CAUTION**
- Medium ownership (15-25%)
- Decent projection but pricey
- Popular but potentially necessary

#### **🔴 RED = AVOID IN TOURNAMENTS**
- High ownership (>30%)
- Low projection for the price
- Chalk plays everyone will have

---

### **💰 Quick Decision Framework:**

**Ask yourself these 3 questions:**

1. **"Will lots of people play this player?"** 
   - If YES → Avoid in tournaments (unless elite projection)
   - If NO → Consider strongly

2. **"Is the projection high for the price?"**
   - Check the Value score
   - Higher = better

3. **"Does this help me be different?"**
   - Look at ownership %
   - Under 10% = great differentiation

---

### **🎲 Sample Tournament Strategy:**

1. **Pick 1 expensive stud** with low ownership from Opportunities tab
2. **Pick 1 team stack** from Stacks tab (3-4 players)
3. **Fill remaining spots** with contrarian targets under 8% ownership
4. **Avoid anyone** over 25% ownership unless they're must-plays

**Total budget:** $50,000 (DraftKings) or $60,000 (FanDuel)

---

### **📊 What the Numbers Mean:**

- **Ownership 5%** = Almost nobody playing them (contrarian gold)
- **Ownership 15%** = Decent play, not too popular
- **Ownership 30%** = Very popular (chalk)
- **Leverage 1.0+** = Excellent tournament play
- **Projection 15+** = Strong scoring expectation for hitters
- **Projection 30+** = Strong scoring expectation for pitchers
